Why it exists

The structural condition it responds to.

Constraint moved upstream

In many transition environments, invention is not the limiter. The limiter is the ability to assemble a coherent pathway: evidence, adoption logic, execution capacity, and institutional coordination.

When that structure is missing, projects accumulate activity without convergence. They look alive, but they do not become investable, scalable, or governable.

The Composer exists to force convergence early — by making constraint and exposure legible while sequencing is still adjustable.
