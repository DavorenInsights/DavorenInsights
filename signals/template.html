Signal Title

One clear sentence framing what this signal reveals.

Feb 2026 • 3 min

Innovation Architectures • Futures • Strategic Systems

Observation

State what is happening. No drama. No exaggeration. Just clarity.

Interpretation

Why this matters beneath the surface. What pressure created this? What is shifting structurally? What most observers are missing.

Strategic Implication

What this changes across the system. Who benefits? Who is exposed? What accelerates or stalls?

Strategic Note

One or two sharp sentences. Almost aphoristic. Example: “Transitions rarely fail from lack of ambition — they fail from coordination debt.”
