What this is

Davoren Insights is a small, high-signal publishing surface for people working inside complex transitions: energy systems, industrial policy, climate programmes, technology adoption, and execution risk.

The focus is not commentary. It’s decision-ready clarity: what’s changing, why it matters, where failure modes cluster, and what to watch next.
